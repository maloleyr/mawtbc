# 2026 Training Hike Schedule - West Michigan

Group hikes will be every other weekend. On the off weekends you should still get out and hike a few miles. Challenge yourself to meet or exceed the upcoming miles goal. During the week rest, take short walks, and don’t forget to stretch!

West Michigan is blessed to have so many opportunities for hikes. Our hikes will take us primarily along the North Country Trail (use these miles towards your [Hike 100 Challenge](https://northcountrytrail.org/hike-100-challenge/)) to best prepare us for Hike Weekend. This is West Michigan orientated and some travel time will be expected. Most hikes will be out-and-back, meaning that we will hike out and then return back to our vehicles along the same stretch of trail. 

Severe weather such as thunderstorms or temperatures in excess of 90 degrees fahrenheit in the forecast will result in cancellation of that scheduled training hike.

## Leader Rules: Please follow these additional instructions from your Hike Leader.

1. Carry at least 2 liters of water on your person.
2. No Cotton! Wear synthetic materials or wool.
3. Weather can be unpredictable so please pack accordingly. This means that we can, and likely will, continue to hike through rain.

# Make a Wish Trailblaze Challenge
Make a Wish Trailblaze Challenge. A collection of my routes and tips for training each summer.

## Huh? 

The Trailblaze Challenge is a fundraising event that I participate in annually with Make a Wish of Michigan. We spend the summer training (via training hikes), raising money, and then culminating in a large group hike of 22-24 miles in the fall. 

This repository will hold the individual hike plans and the overall schedule.
