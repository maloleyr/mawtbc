## Plan

Meet at the Croton Dam (boat launch at bottom). This is a wonderful hike through several different forests and meadows. See if you can spot my favorite campsite! This will be the longest training hike before Hike Weekend. Put in the effort and you’ll make it. We’ll take a lunch break at the creek. This hike incorporates a good chunk of our Hike Weekend hike route as well.
